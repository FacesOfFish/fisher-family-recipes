Grandmumsie's Terriyaki Pot Roast

Ingredients

  • 3-4 lb. Pot Roast
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger
  • 1 (or more) Clove Garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 Onions, sliced
  • 1 Cup Beef Broth

Steps

  1. Place roast in crockpot.
  2. Sprinkle with ginger and garlic.
  3. Arrange onions on top. Pour broth and soy sauce over.
  4. Cook on low 10-12 hours, or high 4-5 hours.
  5. Turn occasionally to keep moist.

Sweet Bean Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1lb (or less) Minced Beef
  • 2 Can (15oz) Baked Beans
  • Chopped Onion to taste
  • 1/2 Cup Molasses (or to taste)
  • 1/3 Cup Srown Sugar
  • 1/3 Cup Ketchup

Steps

  1. Brown beef and onion
  2. Drain Fat. Add beans, molasses, sugar and ketchup. Stir.
  3. Top with bacon bits, if desired
  4. (Can be heated stove top, or in a slow cooker (low, 3-4 hrs), or oven (30 mins at 350f, 180c)

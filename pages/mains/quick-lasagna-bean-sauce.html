Quick Lasagna with Bean Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Oil
  • 2 Tsp. Minced Garlic
  • 1 Small Onion, Finely Chopped
  • 2 Cups Cooked Bean (Red, Brown, Black; Whatever) Drained and Coarsely Chopped
  • 4 Cups Tomato Puree
  • 1 Tsp. Oregano
  • 1 Tsp. Dried Basil

Steps

  1. Heat oil.
  2. Saute onion and garlic one minute.
  3. Add beans.
  4. Cook and STIR for four minutes.
  5. Add tomato puree, oegano, and basil
  6. Boil, reduce heat and simmer five minutes
  7. 3/4lb

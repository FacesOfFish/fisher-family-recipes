Apple Salad

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Apples of your choice, diced
  • 1 Large Banana, diced
  • 1/3 cup Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 cup Raisins
  • 1/3 cup Peanuts
  • 1/3 cup Mayo or Salad Dressing
  • 1/3 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice
  • 1/4 cup Half and Half (Light Cream in UK)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine apples, banana, raisins, and peanuts
  2. In a small bowl, mix mayo, peanut butter, vanilla, lemon juice, half and half (light cream), and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Just before serving, toss the fruit with the dressing.

Tater Tot Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Hot Breakfast Sausage
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 1 Bell Pepper, chopped (red is colorful)
  • 1 Dozen Large Eggs
  • 1 Tbsp Hot Sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Pepper
  • 32 oz. Bag Tater Tots, Frozen
  • 8 oz. (about 2 Cups) Shredded Colby Jack Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Milk

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350f (180c)
  2. Brown sausage for 5 minutes. Drain
  3. Add onion and pepper. Saute until soft.
  4. Whisk together eggs, milk, hot sauce, salt and pepper in a large bowl
  5. Put tater tots in an even layer in a greased 3 quart casserole dish.
  6. Top with 1 cup cheese and the sausage mixture
  7. Pour egg mixture over all and top with remaining 1 cup cheese.
  8. Bake 50-55 mins.
  9. Remove from heat, and let stand 10 minutes before serving.

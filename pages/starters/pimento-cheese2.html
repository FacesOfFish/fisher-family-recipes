Pimento Cheese

(Moms recipe)

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Flavorful Block Chedder (such as Boar's Head white cheddar with horseradish)
  • 2 oz. Jar Diced Pimentos, drained
  • 6 tbsp Mayo
  • 1/2 tsp Chili powder
  • 1/8 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/8 tsp Cumin
  • 1/4 tsp Cayenne Red Pepper

Steps

  1. Grate the cheddar
  2. Add everythign else and stir lightly
  3. (I stir after adding each spice. It mixes better.)
  4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving on Hawaiian Bread (sweet bread)

Pimento Cheese

(Stedman Cookbook)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Velvetta
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 Cup White Vinegar
  • 3 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 Large Can Pimento, Mashed
  • 1 Cup Mayo

Steps

  1. Melt cheese and milk in double boiler until smooth
  2. Add all remaining ingredients except mayo.
  3. Cook for 30 mins
  4. Remove from heat, let cool, and stir in mayo
